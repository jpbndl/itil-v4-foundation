# Continual Improvement Model

## Continual Improvement Plan

A model providing a structured approach for implementing improvements.

Helps link improvements to overall goals, missions and objectives

### 7 Steps

1. What is the Vision?
2. Where are we now?
3. Where do we want to be?
4. How do we get there?
5. Take action.
6. Did we get there?
7. How do we maintain momentum?

## 1. What is the Vision?

As measured by business goals/mission/objectives

## 2. Where are we now?

Baseline Assessments

## 3. Where do we want to be?

Metrics:

- Specific
- Measureable
- Achievable
- Relevant
- Time Bound

## 4. How do we get there?

- Service Improvement Plans
- Process Improvement Plans

## 5. Take action

Execution of Improvements

## 6. Did we get there?

Validation of metrics

## 7. How do we maintain momentum?

- Market
- Reinforce
